myzoo

myzoo is a platform that allows local offline businesses to connect out to their target clients while ensuring quick product & service delivery. A hyperlocal business's service radius could be anything from a few meters to a few kilometers from its physical location.In the chosen locations or zones, ZOO will provide the quickest delivery possible from nearby or local suppliers. Consumers or end-users expect fresh and consumable products to arrive as soon as possible. ZOO will expressly assist your demand and business growth.

How does ZOO Work?

The operation of ZOO is straightforward.

Here's how it goes:

• Sellers list their products on the marketplace's website or mobile app.

• Customers explore and add things to their shopping carts.

• The customer can pay by credit card or cash-on-delivery.

• The order is received by the seller via the marketplace website/app.

• After that, the order is accepted by the merchant.

• They pack the order to protect it from damage during shipping and transportation.

• A delivery agent comes to the store to pick up the purchase.

• The order is delivered to the customer's door.

Customers and sellers have separate apps or website fronts with varied functionality based on their needs in these arrangements. Orders are completed promptly with this method, and customers can receive order tracking information within the app, as well as via email and SMS.
